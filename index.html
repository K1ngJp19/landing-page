Jackson Goldstone

Jackson Goldstone is a Canadian cyclist from Squamish,
British Columbia competing in Downhill mountain biking.
He won the Junior Downhill 2021 UCI Mountain Bike World
Championships in Val di Sole, Italy and the 2022 Junior World
Cup Overall title.
